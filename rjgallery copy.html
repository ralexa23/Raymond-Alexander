*Rj's Audio Assigment*

The audio depicts a tense warzone scenario in which a unit strategically battles their enemies to capture the city. I combined five different audio pieces, including various sound samples and my own voice. To enhance the realism of the composition, I utilized effects such as distortion, amplification, and echo. The most difficult aspect of the assignment was mastering the application of these effects, but through experimentation, I learned how to use them effectively and precisely.
